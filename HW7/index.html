    • The setting takes place in a fantasy world where monsters, magic, and other fantasy-like elements exist. The story begins in a humble village by the forest where our main character lives. One day, when he is in the forest, he stumbles upon a strange-looking slime that was unconscious. The slime suddenly wakes up and upon seeing the MC, says, “You must be the hero I am looking for!” Through much deliberation, the MC reluctantly takes on the hero role and with the slime that has no other memories, they journey to save the world(?). However, what the hero did know is that in this world, monsters cannot directly fight people due to a curse and so they fight through challenges instead.
